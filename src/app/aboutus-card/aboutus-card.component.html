ABOUT US




Hearty, healthy, homely


Escape the proving ground, take a pause at The Proofing Ground. In a world that demands us to constantly prove our worth, we offer a welcome respite to escape the daily grind and savor the simple pleasures of hearty, healthy, and homemade food. We invite you to slow down, connect with loved ones, and rediscover the joy of shared experiences through the warm embrace of home.

Our menu features comforting classics crafted with care, using only the finest ingredients to nourish hearts and palates. Enjoy our hand-kneaded sourdoughs and shokupan milk breads with curated compound butters, or indulge in our probiotic-rich kombucha and milk kefir - all freshly made on order.
